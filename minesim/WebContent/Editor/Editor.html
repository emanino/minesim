Your task is to "capture" the meaning of a sentence.

  • The first time you do this task, please take a few minutes to read through the instructions and watch the videos.
    It should take between 5 and 10 minutes.
  • Use the arrows on the left and right to navigate through the instructions.
  • On the last page, you will be able to complete the task and submit your result.
  • On the last page there will also be a link back to read these instructions again.
  • Important: To complete this task you need to have a basic understanding of variables.
This quick video will give you an idea of how to "capture" the meaning of a sentence.
Step by step instructions:
  1. Read carefully the sentence. Your task is to capture its meaning using the sentence snippets provided.For example: "The menu of vegan restaurant A contains a tofu dish".
  2. Search suitable snippets using the search option provided. For example, there might a snippet to describe a ""vegan restaurant".
  3. Once you have chosen a snippet, drag and drop it inside "Your solution" box.
  4. If the sentence given to you is an "if-then" sentence, recreate it using the "if" and "then" snippets.
  5. Most snippets require you to select variables, such as "A", "B" and "C".
  6. If the sentence you are given contains variables, you must use them in your snippets accordingly.
  7. If instead of a variable you want to specify a specific value (such as a number), choose the option "[...]" in the variable selection.
  8. Once you are satisfied that you captured the meaning of the sentence well, click on "Submit your answer".
  9. If you think it is not possible to capture the meaning of the sentence, click on "There is no good answer". You will be asked to specify which part of the sentence is problematic.
Example of dealing with an "if-then" sentence.
Example of what to do when it is not possible to capture the meaning of a sentence.
Important hints:
  1. When searching for snippets, try using synonyms. For example, you might not find a snippet that contains "more", but you can find one that contains "greater".
  2. If your search finds many results, you might have to scroll down to see them all.
  3. Don't worry if your solution contains some small grammatical mistakes.
  4. If you make a mistake, you can always delete selected snippets by clicking on the bin icon on the right.
  5. Please make an effort! If you submit random answers they will be rejected and you will be banned from further HITs.
